Social Determinants of Health

A fundamental analysis of the relationship between socioeconomic factors and population's health

Data Description

The data was sourced from Country Health Rankings. It includes health, economic, and demographic characteristics measured at the county level in the US. There are a total of 25 columns with data from the following categories:

  • Data identifiers
  • Outcome variables
    • The outcome variable I chose to examine was Poor Health, which indicates the proportion of county that has a poor health status
  • Social determinants of health
    • Behavioral
    • Environmental
    • Demographic

An additional column "Region" was created based on the "State Abbreviation" column.

Objective

Looking at the data made available to me, I set out three guiding questions that oriented my analyses:

  1. Which social determinants of health is the most significant predictor of the health index?
  2. How does socioeconomic level affect the health index?
  3. How does access/quality of care impact the health index?
