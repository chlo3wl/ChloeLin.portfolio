About Me

A glimpse into my life

I am a first-generation college student at the University of Pennsylvania studying Business Analytics. I am fueled by my passion for understanding the deeper meanings behind a vast amount of quantatitive and qualitative information. On campus, I work closely with the Wharton Undergraduate Entrepreneurship Club to promote entrepreneurial spirit and venture-focused thinking.

Most recently, I was a Business Operations intern at Vestwell, a VC-backed FinTech startup in NYC. Using Python and SQL, I automated a file generation process pulling data from a Postgre SQL server and transforming them into cleaned CSV files. My work helped to save ~80 hours of manual work, drastically reducing the team's project timeline.
